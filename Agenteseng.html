Introduction

Agents are the characters that the players can use in the game, once you choose one you cannot change your choice for the rest of the game.

They are divided in 4 different classes. Duelists, Sentinels, Initiators, and Controllers.

Duelists

They are usually the most popular and difficult characters to control, their job is usually to infiltrate in the enemy area to kill someone silently or take advantage of their aggressive abilities to enter the enemy area with their team.

Sentinels

Sentinels are defensive characters, focused on blocking areas, especially in defense rounds, they alone are capable of controlling an entire area, preventing enemies from passing until their teammates arrive.

Initiators

Initiators are the first to enter the battle, and their abilities are focused on making that task easier for them: Stuns, blinders, and more similar abilities to disable certain abilities of enemies and make the entry easier.

Controllers

They are in charge of dividing the terrain into several zones, blocking enemy vision and clearing the way for their team. Their abilities allow them, among other things, to place smokes in the form of walls or spheres.
